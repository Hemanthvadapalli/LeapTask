Various Kinds of Plants in NIT Trichy

S.no Plant Description
1 Millettia pinnata(pungai) Millettia pinnata is well-adapted to arid zones and has many traditional uses.It is often used for landscaping as a windbreak or for shade due to the large canopy and showy, fragrant flowers.
2 Terminalia arjuna(Neeramarudha) Arjun is one of the species whose leaves are fed by the moth Antheraea paphia, which produces tassar silk, a wild silk of commercial importance.
3 Jamun(Naaval) Jamun is originally from India. Its tree is tall and evergreen. Therefore, it is generally grown as an alley or as a windbreak. Although the fruits are liked by everyone and sold at a high price, it is still not cultivated as an orchard tree. Jamun is found all over India. Jamun fruits are a good source of iron and are said to be helpful in heart and liver problems. Jamun seeds are an effective anti-diabetic medicine and their powder is widely used in India to control diabetes.
